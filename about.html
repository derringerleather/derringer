About Derringer Leather


Since 1985, Derringer Leather has been a proud subsidiary of UNIK International, a renowned industry leader dedicated to serving the motorcycle community across North America and Europe. Leveraging decades of expertise in leather tanning and manufacturing, Derringer Leather epitomizes quality and craftsmanship in motorcycle apparel and accessories.


At Derringer Leather, every product is meticulously crafted using hand-selected leather and premium textiles, ensuring durability, comfort, and style. Our commitment to excellence is evident in the meticulous supervision and stringent quality control measures applied throughout the manufacturing process.


Headquartered in New Jersey, Derringer Leather operates from a state-of-the-art distribution center, facilitating prompt delivery of our products to customers nationwide. We believe in making high-quality motorcycle gear accessible to all enthusiasts without compromising on quality or affordability.

Beyond our products, Derringer Leather is dedicated to fostering enduring relationships with our customers. We prioritize customer satisfaction by offering superior products and unwavering support, backed by the trusted legacy of UNIK International. Our commitment to quality and service means that when you choose Derringer Leather, you're choosing gear that stands the test of time and rides with you every mile.
